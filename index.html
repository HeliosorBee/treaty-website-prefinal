Treaty Two

What is Treaty Two

Treaty Two known as the “Manitoba Post Treaty” was made on August 21, 1871 at Manitoba Post as they were also present and participating in the making of Treaty One. Treaty two was made by five Chiefs: Mekis, Francois (Broken Fingers), Ma-sah-kee-yash, Keweetahquinnayash and Sou-sonce.
- Treaty No.2
